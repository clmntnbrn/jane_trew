My professional story

Profile

For over 12 years I have been realising my clients’ vision; and during six of those my own. As well as being a seasoned business consultant and delivery manager, I am a business owner.

I have cut my teeth on enterprise, doing whatever’s required to elevate brands and propel commercial interests. Generating new ideas that either improve existing products, or create the impetus for new ones.

My creative mind is shoulder to shoulder with my business - originator of creative briefs, adding colour and keeping it fresh, I am a participant in the inventive journey working in close alliance with creative teams.

Having worked autonomously within an array of business environments to deliver change, I am self-motivated, flexible and versatile.

Spearheading full delivery lifecycle and taking strategic ideas through to successful outcomes, I’m considered in my approach and dedicated in my performance.
As a natural solution provider with a healthy business acumen and multifaceted experience, I am an advocate of smart business, light footprints and streamlining.

With effective interpersonal skills, I am a devoted brand custodian; championing start-ups, innovators and concepts with a ‘pull-up your sleeves and get stuck in‘ mentality.

Accustomed to the front lines, I can demystify language to accommodate my audience, translating intricate concepts into conversational English – and then into real tangible products that drive measurable results.

Exercising my business intuition, I can always find a way forward. I hold details in high regard and know how to find a story through them - mining insights without losing sight of the big picture.
