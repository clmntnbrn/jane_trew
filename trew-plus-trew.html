Trew+Trew

Trew+Trew was borne from opportunity and a demand of services that materialised under Eve’s Watch consultancy arm of The Eve Group. As a side-benefit from our placement within the horological market, our expertise was being sought out for a number of business initiatives and brands not just within the industry but beyond. As a smart business we were able to respond to this new scope and cater for new enquiries.

As co-director of this integrated multi-disciplined boutique consultancy, I worked with a hub of white-labelled seasoned talent on a project-by-project basis to deliver bespoke client services. This business was a manifestation of my core skill set in perfect harmony with my pioneering spirit, and I enjoyed every second of it.

Friends of Trew+Trew include the fantastic design agency WeAreBeard and the social media gurus SuperDigital.
