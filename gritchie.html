Gritchie Case Study

Gritchie

Trew+Trew’s was retained as brand custodian for Guy Ritchie’s evolving portfolio brand GRITCHIE, responsible for the delivery of multiple projects including business consolidation, creative strategy, brand development and design, marketing and digital services.

Reporting directly to Guy Ritchie, my mission was to translate his vision into ‘on point’ tangible products for commercial endeavours. Guardianship remit included 24/7 ‘fix it’ business support across the portfolio as well as reinforcing the brand infrastructure instilling checks, assurances and governance to protect Guy Ritchie’s interests

I led the creative team in developing the brand identities, stories and narratives for both Gritchie Brewing Company (GBC) and Lore of the Land (LOTL). As part of this, I conceptualised the archangel theme and brand tapestry approach for GBC Lore beer range and supported product placement in films; oversaw and directed the design and production of point-of-sale products, merchandise and beer bottles.

Working with our hand-picked talent, produced websites and social marketing for Gritchie Brewing Company Gritchie Brewing Company and Lore of the Land, undertaking photoshoots to produce visual content as well as written copy to effectively articulate the brand to consumers.
