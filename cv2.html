EXPERTISE

Professional competencies that I can bring to add value for your clients and to your business:


With 12+ years experience delivering projects, account management and business consultancy, along with 6+ years as a business owner, I have highly developed business acumen and a problem-solving mind.

Having worked in both the public and private sector, I have a unique perspective and multi-faceted experience of many aspects of project delivery, including varied approaches to business discovery; cross-party strategy development; new business sourcing; authoring investment and commercial proposals, along with board and consultation papers.

Accustomed to being on the front lines in the fast paced world of digital media, I’ve refined that critical skill of translating intricate, digital concepts into conversational English – and then into real, tangible products that drive measurable results.

My passion for innovation and creative problem solving has always been an asset to the organisations I’ve worked with. I hold details in high regard, and know how to find a story through them, rather than be intimidated by them - mining insights without losing sight of the big picture is a real skill.

Throughout my career working on outward-facing products, I’ve become a passionate advocate for the end customer and feel most at home as a champion in converging business and consumer goals. I’m a natural collaborator and have found I am most effective when working with smart people who are themselves empowered to drive the product vision and strategic approach.
